Since 1985, Cool Store has been the pride and joy of the TownPlace community We have strived to provide the best services to our customers and vendors since then. Over the years, we have refined and improved our ability to address the needs and accessabilities of our customers and vendors. From our humble beginings to our current sprawling selection of stores from boutiques to bouncy castles.

Now, Cool Store stands as the cultural touchstone of TownPlace. A spot for everyone, young and old to congregate and engage withe their fellow neighbors and friends. We aim to make memories and fun above all else, and strive for all avenues that help facilitate this goal.

We would love to see you and invite you to visit us. Anything you need, we've got it!
